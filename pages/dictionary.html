अग्नि

NOUN · MASCULINE

Pronunciation

AG-nee

Meaning

Fire; the god of fire; brightness; heat; a form of energy or life-force.

Etymology

From the root anj (to anoint) or ag (to move), referring to the active nature of fire.

Example Usage

अग्निमीळे पुरोहितं

"I praise Agni, the divine priest..."

— Rigveda 1.1.1

ज्ञान

NOUN · NEUTER

Pronunciation

GYAA-num

Meaning

Knowledge; wisdom; understanding; awareness; the act of knowing.

Etymology

From the root jña (to know), central to Sanskrit philosophy and spiritual exploration.

Example Usage

ज्ञानं परमं गुह्यम्

"Knowledge is the supreme secret..."

— Bhagavad Gita 18.63
